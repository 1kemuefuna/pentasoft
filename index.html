Flagship Product — Sloopcode

Sloopcode is a microjob platform designed to offer software developers a frictionless way to earn money and build their skills while job hunting or upskilling. Unlike traditional freelancing platforms, Sloopcode eliminates interviews, proposals, and client communication barriers.

How It Works

  • Developers browse curated microjobs directly from the platform
  • Jobs are designed and verified by the platform itself — not by clients
  • Developers complete tasks inside Sloophost, our custom cloud-based coding environment
  • Payments are made based on code quality, timeliness, and adherence to the task brief

Key Benefits

  • No proposals or bidding required
  • Instant access to real tasks
  • Earn per completed job — no waiting for client approval
  • Track your performance and grow your developer portfolio
  • Practice real-world coding while earning on the side
